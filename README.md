Features We Built (Veritas AI)

We solved these problems by creating three focused, easy-to-use tools:

1. Deepfake Detector (deepfake.html)

Goal: Tell you if an image or video is fake.
Key Features:
Upload Media: Check any photo or video clip.
AI Score (0-100%): Gives a clear percentage of how much AI was used.
Forensic Report: Explains what signs of fake editing the AI found.
Chatbot: Get advice about AI manipulation.

2. Job Scam Vetting (job\_scam.html)

Goal: Tell you if a job offer is real and safe.
Key Features:
Offer Scan: Paste the job text or email content.
Real-Time Check: Uses live web search to check the company's background.
Clear Verdict: Gives a simple Legitimate, Suspicious, or Scam result.
Evidence Points: Shows the top reasons why the offer passed or failed the check.

3. News Check (news\_check.html)

Goal: Find the truth in confusing news stories.
Key Features:
Multimodal Input: Check both text claims and news screenshots together.
Grounded Analysis: Uses live Google Search results for fact-checking.
Factual Verdict: Gives a simple Real, Fake, or Misleading answer.
Corrections & Sources: If it's fake, the tool gives you the true facts and links to the sources used for verification.
